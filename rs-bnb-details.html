Recommendation System for BNB platform

Recommendation systems have been highly popularized during the past few years by companies like Netflix, Amazon and Airbnb, who have been using these systems in their platforms to provide a better customer experience and improve their sales and revenue.

Some of these organizations have been using collaborative base recommendation systems based on ratings like, the number of stars. However, customers nowadays are more critical at the moment of evaluating products and services. They require more information to take a decision. This is especially important for e-commerce platforms like Lazada and Amazon where customers take important consideration on the reviews of other people in both images and comments to get an understanding about the credibility and quality of a product.

For this project, I sought to solve the question of how we can utilize reviews and comments from other customers to generate a recommendation system instead of using the usual ratings or number of stars.

In this project, I used the data of Airbnb reviews for Singapore which included text reviews of the places where people have stayed. I extracted the sentiments of the reviews and used them as a metric of the product quality. The sentiment was extracted with Vader or Valence Aware Dictionary for Sentiment Reasoning, which was used as a Dictionary for both extracting the polarity and intensity of a text.

Then, I used the Surprise library to generate the recommendation system. Different models were implemented through SVD, KNN based methods and others to identified to provide a potentially better result.

My project provided best results using algorithms like SVD, SVD++ and BaselineOnly based on metrics like MAE and RMSE. K nearest neighbors algorithms were not a great option because of the computation complexity of the algorithm. I still recommend to take a look into these models with other datasets, with more reviews per user. However, since we are using the airbnb dataset where there are few reviews per user, other models are preffered.

SUMMARY

Recommendation systems are vital parts of most of ecommerce and other social platforms that recommend information to users including Amazon, Netflix and Spotify. For this project, the business objective required to identify users with similar reactions toward the platform. I created a recommendation system that used the reviews of users in a text format. I transformed this information using sentiment analysis and creacted a user based recommendation system. This model can help companies identify patterns in the customer reviews to increase their system and customer experience.
